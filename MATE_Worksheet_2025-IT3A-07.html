Task 4 - Analyze
Task 4 Vague vs. Specific
Level: Analyze
One of the key areas from your feedback was being more specific in your analysis. Read each pair of sentences below. For each pair, explain in your own words why Version B is stronger than Version A. Focus on what exactly makes the second version more convincing.
4a

"My AI can help students with their studies and make studying easier."

4b

Identify at least three things that Version B does differently.
